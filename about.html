About Gallery Café

Gallery Café began with one dream — to bring the flavors of the world into a single cozy, heartwarming space. Each dish carries a memory, each aroma carries emotion, and each moment becomes part of our story.

We mix local authenticity with global passion. Whether you enjoy Sri Lankan spices, Arabian grills, Chinese noodles, or French bakery delights, we serve it all with love.
