Our Story

It all began with a spark — a dream to bring the world’s flavors together under one warm roof. From the aromatic streets of Colombo to the bustling kitchens of Paris and Shanghai, we’ve gathered inspiration from every culture that celebrates food with love.

Each dish we serve carries a story, a tradition, and a little bit of home. Our chefs blend local authenticity with international creativity, ensuring every bite feels familiar yet thrillingly new.

Gallery Café isn’t just a place to eat — it’s where conversations brew, friendships rise like warm pastries, and moments linger longer than the aroma of freshly ground coffee.
